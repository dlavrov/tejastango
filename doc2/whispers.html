Crimes and Whispers

July 14 through 30, 2006 at Jawaahir Dance Theatre, Minneapolis

In Buenos Aires there is an ongoing struggle for control over the memory of the disappeared, the thousands of innocent citizens kidnapped and murdered in the 1970's and 80's by a vicious military junta unrepentant in their patriotic defense of national security.  It is a struggle between this same military establishment, the mothers and grandmothers of the disappeared, and a general public blinded by fear and apathy to the tearing asunder of their proud and passionate culture.

Crimes and Whispers is a unique theatrical dance collaboration that fuses the acrobatic modern dance choreography of Gerry Girouard, the expertise of master Tango dancer and Buenos Aires native Florencia Tacetti, and the theatrical imagination of Off-Leash Area (Ivey Award winners and City Pages' 2005 Artists of the Year).
