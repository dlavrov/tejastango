Argentine Tango in Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas

Many people and groups organize Argentine tango activities in the Dallas/Fort Worth area.  This set of links to Argentine tango activities in Dallas and Fort Worth is maintained by Stephen and Susan Brown.

Connecting to the Dallas/Fort Worth Tango Community
1) North Texas Tango Calendars — from Creative Tango
2) Join the Facebook Group: "DFW Tangueros" (membership required)
3) See the Facebook Fan Page Tango in Dallas/Fort Worth Texas - Argentine Tango Milongas and etc (maintained by Jasmine S. Williams)
4) TangoDallas yahoo group (membership required)
