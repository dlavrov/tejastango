About Tejastango.com


Tejastango.com is a website about the dance, music and culture of Argentine Tango, created in 2000 by Stephen and Susan Brown.

Because it was one of a few available documentation on Tango written in English, at a time when interest in Tango was exploding, the website quickly rose to the top of many aspiring tangueros’ reading list.

Due to changing circumstance, Stephen has stopped updating the website since 2013. In 2015 he planned to let the domain expire.

With an interest to keeping this important legacy alive, and to support the rising popularity of tango especially from Asia, our tango school Abrazos Club (in Singapore) have decided to maintain this domain for the next few years.

We are treating this essentially as an Open Source Project. Source pages are now available on Github. Hosting is served from the free Github pages. Like any open source project we plan to collaborate with contributors to develop and improve this resource.

Future plans include: updating the various guides, translation into more languages, and eventually to repurpose the guides into free books for tango teaching around the world.

Please use the Github issue tracker to submit any issues and discussion.

— Kace Ong, Abrazos Club, Singapore
