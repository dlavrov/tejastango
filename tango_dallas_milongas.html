Milongas in Dallas/Fort Worth, Texas

Many people and groups organize Argentine tango activities in the Dallas/Fort Worth area.  We no longer maintain a list milongas in the Dallas/Fort Worth area.

For information on milongas in the Dallas/Fort Worth area, see
1) North Texas Tango Calendars — from Creative Tango
2) the Facebook Group: "DFW Tangueros" (membership required)
3) the Facebook Fan Page Tango in Dallas/Fort Worth Texas - Argentine Tango Milongas and etc (maintained by Jasmine S. Williams)
4) the TangoDallas yahoo group (membership required)
